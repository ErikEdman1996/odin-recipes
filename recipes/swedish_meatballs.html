Swedish Meatballs

A classic Swedish dish served with mashed potatoes, gravy and lingonberry jam!

Swedish meatballs is a dish beloved by many across the globe. With relatively cheap ingredients this staple in Swedish cuisine can become one weekday meal you keep coming back to!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 dl whole milk
  • 5 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 500 g minced meat
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Mix milk and breadcrumbs in a large bowl. Let it sit for 10 min, then add the minced meat, finely chopped onions, egg, salt, black pepper and sugar to the mix. Work it until everything is combined.
  2. Wet your hands using cold water and begin shaping your meatballs to desired size. Fry the meatballs in butter on medium-high heat in batches until cooked through.
  3. Serve your meatballs with either boiled or mashed potatoes, gravy, lingonberry jam and thinly slices pickled cucumber.
