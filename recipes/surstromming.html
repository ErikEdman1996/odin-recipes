Surströmming

Fermented herring, a tradition even some Swedes politely refuse to partake in but oh so delicious with the right mindset!

Fermented herring is best served on a crisp bread together with boiled potatoes, sour cream and diced red onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 1 can fermented herring
  • 2 red onions
  • 1/2 dl thinly chopped chives
  • butter
  • crisp bread
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Wash and boil the potatoes in salted water.
  2. Open the can of fermented herring, preferably outside!
  3. Chop the red onions into small dice. Cut the boiled potatoes into rounds.
  4. Clean the fermented herring by removing the bones and skin.
  5. Butter a crisp bread, add potatoes in one layer, then the herring, red onion and chives. Finish by putting sour cream on top.
